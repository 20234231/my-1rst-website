About Hiroyuki Sawano

Hiroyuki Sawano (澤野 弘之, Sawano Hiroyuki, born September 12, 1980) is a Japanese composer, arranger, lyricist, pianist, and music producer best known for his work on many anime series, video games, television dramas, and movies. His works include the musical scores for Attack on Titan, The Seven Deadly Sins, Blue Exorcist, Guilty Crown, Kill la Kill, Seraph of the End, and Mobile Suit Gundam Unicorn.[1] In addition to his role on the soundtrack, Sawano also composes many opening and ending theme songs. Overall, he has already provided the music for over 100 visual media works.[2] Sawano was nominated for the Newtype Anime Awards 11 times, winning 4 of the nominations.[3] He has also won the Tokyo Anime Award three times,[3][4] as well as two prizes at the 2022 Anime Trending Awards.[5] Sawano has two additional music projects: In 2014, he started a vocal project under the name "SawanoHiroyuki[nZk]" with a focus on collaborating with other artists. In 2022, he formed a new group called "NAQT VANE" with Japanese singer Harukaze and design group Classic 6, focusing on creating music for overseas audiences.
